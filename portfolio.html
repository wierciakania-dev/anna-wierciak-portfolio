Approach

I work at the intersection of research, writing, systems thinking, and generative AI. My focus is not simply producing content faster, but building reliable workflows that transform complex information into clear, structured knowledge.

Rather than treating AI as a replacement for human expertise, I use it as a collaborative thinking partner. It accelerates research, helps organise information, challenges assumptions, improves clarity, and supports iterative refinement while human judgement remains central throughout the process.

My work spans long-form writing, technical documentation, workflow design, knowledge management, and independent research into attention, cognition, behavioural change, and learning.

Every project begins with understanding the problem rather than searching for the fastest answer. Final decisions, reasoning, and editorial judgement always remain human.
